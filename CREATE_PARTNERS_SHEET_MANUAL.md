# 📋 Create Partners Sheet - Manual Setup (2 Minutes)

## ❌ Problem
You're getting a 500 error because the "Partners" sheet doesn't exist in your Google Sheets.

## ✅ Solution
Create the "Partners" sheet manually (takes 2 minutes!)

---

## Step 1: Open Your Google Sheet

**Click this link:**
```
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1rqqMJDLreg8GloJfAT_er6pvB0KhYDSI6GmSg3k2eGM/edit
```

---

## Step 2: Create "Partners" Sheet

1. **Click the "+" button** at the bottom left (next to sheet tabs)
2. **Right-click the new sheet** → Rename → Type: `Partners`

---

## Step 3: Add Headers

**Click cell A1** and paste these headers (copy everything below):

```
Site ID	Partner Name	Company Type	Industry	GST Number	PAN Number	Primary Contact Name	Primary Contact Email	Primary Contact Phone	Secondary Contact Name	Secondary Contact Email	Secondary Contact Phone	Address Line 1	City	State	Pincode	Address Line 2	Contract Start Date	Contract End Date	Contract Value	Billing Cycle	Payment Terms	Service Type	Required Headcount	Shift Type	Working Hours	Working Days	Weekly Off	Performance Rating	Outstanding Amount	Last Payment Date	Status	Remarks
```

**Important**: Make sure you copy the TAB-separated text above. It should spread across 33 columns (A to AG).

---

## Step 4: Format Headers

1. **Select row 1** (click on the row number "1")
2. **Make Bold**: Click the Bold button or press Ctrl+B
3. **Background Color**: Click fill color → Choose Blue
4. **Text Color**: Click text color → Choose White

---

## ✅ Done!

Now go back to your app and try adding a partner again. It should work!

---

## 🔍 What's the Difference Between Clients and Partners?

Looking at your app, it seems you have:

### **Clients Tab**
- Uses "Clients" sheet
- Simplified client management
- Focus on agency margins and billing

### **Partners Tab**
- Uses "Partners" sheet
- Detailed partner/site management
- More fields for contracts, performance, etc.

**You can use either one depending on your needs!**

---

## 💡 Quick Fix Alternative

If you don't need the Partners tab, you can:

1. Just use the **Clients tab** for all your client management
2. The Clients tab already works and has all the features you need
3. Ignore the Partners tab for now

---

## 🚨 After Creating the Sheet

1. **Refresh your app**: Press Ctrl + Shift + R
2. **Try adding a partner** in the Partners tab
3. **Should work now!** ✅
