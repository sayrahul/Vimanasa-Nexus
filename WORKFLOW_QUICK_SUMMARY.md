## 🎯 4-Step Business Process

### 1️⃣ Add Client (One-time)
**Go to**: Clients tab → Click "Add New Client"

**Fill in**:
- Client name, GST, location
- Contact person details
- Payment terms (Net 30, Net 45)
- **Agency Margin**: 8.5% or 10%
- Contract dates

**Result**: Client ready for employee deployment

---

### 2️⃣ Deploy Employee (One-time per employee)
**Go to**: Workforce tab → Select employee → Click "Deploy to Client"

**Set**:
- Select client
- **Pay Rate** (what you pay): ₹28,000
- System auto-calculates:
  - Employer statutory: ₹4,270
  - Agency commission: ₹2,743
  - **Bill Rate** (what you charge): ₹35,013
  - With GST: ₹41,315

**Result**: Employee deployed, ready for attendance

---

### 3️⃣ Daily Operations (Every day)
**Employees**:
- Mark attendance (Punch In/Out)
- Apply for leaves if needed

**Admin**:
- Monitor attendance
- Approve/reject leaves

**Result**: Daily records maintained

---

### 4️⃣ Monthly Billing (End of month)
**Day 30/31**: Lock attendance

**Day 1-2**: Generate invoices
- Go to Invoices tab
- Select client
- Select month
- Click "Generate Invoice"
- Download PDF
- Send to client

**Day 3-5**: Generate payroll
- Go to Finance tab
- Generate employee payslips
- Disburse salaries

**Day 5-30**: Track payments
- Mark invoices as paid when received

**Result**: Monthly cycle complete!
