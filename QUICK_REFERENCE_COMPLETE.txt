📊 QUICK ACTIONS:

MARK ATTENDANCE:
1. Click "Attendance" tab
2. Select date
3. Mark status for each employee
4. Click "Save Attendance"

SUBMIT LEAVE:
1. Click "Leave" tab
2. Click "New Leave Request"
3. Fill form
4. Submit

SUBMIT EXPENSE:
1. Click "Expenses" tab
2. Click "New Expense Claim"
3. Fill form
4. Submit

GENERATE PAYSLIPS:
1. Click "Payroll" tab
2. Click "Generate Payslips" (purple button)
3. Select month
4. Select employee (or "All")
5. Click "Generate PDF"

EXPORT DATA:
1. Go to any table
2. Click Download icon
3. Choose format (Excel/CSV/Print)
